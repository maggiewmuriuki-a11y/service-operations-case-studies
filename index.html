Service Operations Case Studies

This page highlights real examples of my work in managing SLA performance, handling escalations, and improving team and service delivery outcomes across international environments.

📊 SLA Management Across 17 Countries

Context: Service delivery spanned 17 African countries with varying performance levels and operational challenges.

Challenge: Inconsistent SLA adherence due to differences in processes, communication, and workload distribution.

Action:

  • Monitored SLA performance trends using ServiceNow
  • Worked directly with Country Managers and Team Leads
  • Identified performance gaps and recommended corrective actions
  • Supported teams in managing backlog and high-risk cases

Result:

  • Improved SLA consistency across multiple regions
  • Reduced backlog and delayed cases
  • Strengthened cross-country collaboration

⚠️ Escalation & Complaint Management

Context: Managed high-risk and sensitive customer escalations across multiple regions.

Challenge: Customers were frustrated due to delays and unresolved issues, requiring both technical resolution and strong communication.

Action:

  • Took ownership of escalated cases end-to-end
  • Communicated clearly and consistently with customers
  • Coordinated with internal teams to resolve root causes
  • Applied structured de-escalation techniques

Result:

  • Resolved complex cases successfully
  • Maintained customer trust and satisfaction
  • Reduced repeat escalations

👥 Team Coaching & Performance Improvement

Context: Team members required support to handle complex cases and meet performance expectations.

Challenge: Inconsistent handling approaches led to performance gaps and increased escalations.

Action:

  • Trained new hires on systems and workflows
  • Coached team members on complex case handling
  • Shared best practices and guidance materials

Result:

  • Faster onboarding and improved readiness
  • Increased team confidence and capability
  • More consistent service delivery
